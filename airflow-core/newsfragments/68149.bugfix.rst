Fix a scheduler crash and a log-path mismatch when rendering ``log_filename_template`` for runs without a ``logical_date`` (asset-triggered or partitioned runs, AIP-83). The scheduler-side renderer now resolves the timestamp the same way the log reader does (``logical_date or run_after``) and exposes ``ts``, so worker-written and UI-read log paths stay consistent and the scheduler no longer dereferences a ``None`` ``logical_date``.
